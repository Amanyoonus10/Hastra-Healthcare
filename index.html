Hemostasis & Closure

03
Labor Induction

Cervical Ripening Balloon

Dual balloon Mechanical dilation Non-drug

Flexible shaft Outpatient use Low profile

Articulating tip Laparoscopic Reusable handle

Post-Surgical Care

Adhesion Prevention Barrier Gel

A resorbable gel barrier applied at the close of pelvic and abdominal surgery to reduce the formation of post-operative adhesions between tissue surfaces.

Resorbable Gel format Applied at closure
