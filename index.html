OUR APPROACH

How We Conduct Business

Integrity in Action.
Trust in Every Step.

Our processes are built to ensure ethical practices, compliance, and long-term trust across every partnership.

Regulatory Compliance – Zero Shortcuts

All products are registered under CDSCO before commercial launch.

  • All products registered under CDSCO before commercial launch — no pre-registration sales
  • CDSCO loan licence and MD-14 import licences filed simultaneously, Month 1
  • Post-market vigilance protocols active from Day 1 of commercial operations
  • Adverse event reporting to CDSCO within mandated timelines, no suppression
  • Annual ISO 13485 OEM facility audits — third-party, not self-declared

Partner & Sub-dealer Commitments

Written agreements with clear commission structures and timelines.

  • Written sub-dealer agreements with clear commission structure before first order
  • No retrospective changes to dealer margins without 60-day written notice
  • CRM-based pipeline tracking — no adversarial reporting relationships
  • On-time payment commitment: sub-dealers paid within 30 days of their collections
  • Product training and IFU provided before sub-dealer stocks any Hastra product

Anti-Bribery & Anti-Corruption

Zero facilitation payments. Strict adherence to law and ethical conduct.

  • Zero facilitation payments to any government official — GEM/tender process followed as documented
  • No cash incentives to prescribers in any form — CME sponsorship follows FOGSI guidelines
  • All hospitality at conferences within documented OPPI-compliant limits
  • Conflicts of interest disclosed by all Hastra employees in writing annually

Supply Chain Ethics

Transparent sourcing. Labour compliance. Environmentally responsible.

  • OEM manufacturers audited against ISO 13485 before onboarding — not just certified
  • No child labour or forced labour in supply chain — ILO core standards required in all OEM contracts
  • Environmental disposal compliance: single-use devices disposed per BMWM Rules 2016
  • Honest representation of Indian OEM status — no misrepresentation of country of origin

Patient Rights & Commitments

  • Easy access to product info and safety guidelines.
  • Clear grievance redressal within 30 days.
  • Respect for patient consent, privacy & dignity.
  • No denial of essential care due to financial constraints.
  • We do not market directly to patients.
